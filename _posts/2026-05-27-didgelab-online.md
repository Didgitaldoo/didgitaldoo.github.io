# Launched DidgeLab online

We launched DidgeLab online a few months ago. You can design the shape that you want, put it in DidgeLab and the software will find a didgeridoo shape with this sound. It is free to use, but please consider leaving a donation.

[https://didgelab.com/](https://didgelab.com)

We put a lot of effort in the [documentation](https://didgelab.com/docs).
