About Didgelabs

DidgeLab, created by Jan Nehring, is a free toolkit for computing didgeridoo geometries. It offers acoustical simulation to compute resonant frequencies and computational evolution to design didgeridoo shapes with specific sonic properties. It provides powerful features for didgeridoo builders and researchers to explore the acoustics of didgeridoos and to design new instruments with specific sound properties. link: https:///
